## About Page Content

### Company Story
Tidurlah Grafika is a printing and merchandise company based in Bandar Lampung, specializing in custom printing services and merchandise. As the parent company, Tidurlah Grafika serves as the foundation for specialized services, including ID Card Lampung.

ID Card Lampung operates as a specialized division focusing exclusively on ID card products, lanyards, and related services. While maintaining its own brand identity and expertise in ID card solutions, ID Card Lampung is part of the Tidurlah Grafika family of services.

### Mission
To provide high-quality, creative, and fast printing and merchandise services to customers in Lampung and beyond, with a focus on customer satisfaction and product excellence.

### Vision
To be the leading printing and merchandise service provider in Lampung, known for quality, creativity, and exceptional customer service.

## Usage Guidelines

### When to Use Each Brand
- **Tidurlah Grafika**: General merchandise, promotional materials, general printing services, company-wide communications
- **ID Card Lampung**: ID card specific products, lanyard services, ID card related inquiries, specialized ID card promotions

### Brand Co-Existence
Both brands can be mentioned together when appropriate, but always clarify the relationship:
- "ID Card Lampung, bagian dari Tidurlah Grafika"
- "Tidurlah Grafika, dengan ID Card Lampung untuk layanan ID card khusus"
